# Warranty

Sourced from the live Warranty page (`src/app/(storefront)/support/warranty/page.tsx`).

Every device ships with a **minimum 30-day warranty**. Full legal terms live on the Warranty Policy page — link the customer there for specifics beyond this summary.

## What's covered

Full functionality of the device as described on its product page — including the components tested during inspection (battery, screen, ports, keyboard/trackpad, and overall performance).

## What's not covered

Accidental damage after delivery, unauthorized repairs, liquid damage, and normal cosmetic wear that matches the listed condition grade.

## Extended warranty

Some listings offer an extended warranty option at checkout. Availability and terms vary by listing — check the specific product page, don't assume every device has this option.

## How to file a claim

1. Contact support with the order number and a description of the issue.
2. Support walks through basic troubleshooting, or confirms the device needs to be sent in.
3. If a repair, replacement, or refund is needed, a prepaid shipping label is provided.

Never state warranty coverage more broadly than what's listed here — if a customer asks about something not covered above (e.g. a specific accidental-damage scenario), say what's known and offer to escalate rather than guessing which way it would be judged.
