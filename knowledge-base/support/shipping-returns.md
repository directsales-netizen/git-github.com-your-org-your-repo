# Shipping & Returns

Sourced from the live Shipping Info and Returns pages (`src/app/(storefront)/support/shipping/page.tsx`, `.../returns/page.tsx`).

## Shipping

- **Processing time**: 1–2 business days.
- **Delivery time**: 3–7 business days.
- Ships from the business's Austin, TX facility (exact address comes from live Business Settings, not hardcoded here).
- A tracking number is emailed as soon as an order ships; status is also visible in the customer's account.
- Every device is packed with padding around it and its original accessories where included.
- If a package is delayed, lost, or arrives damaged, tell the customer to contact support promptly rather than chase the carrier alone.

Never promise a specific delivery *date* — only this general processing/delivery window, unless a tool result gives an exact tracking-based estimate.

## Returns

1. Sign in, find the order under order history.
2. Select the item, choose "Start a Return."
3. Print the prepaid return label emailed after starting the return, pack the device securely.
4. Drop off with the listed carrier — a confirmation email follows once it's received.

A customer without an account, or who needs help, should be directed to contact support to start the return for them.

**What to include**: the device plus everything it originally shipped with (charger, cables, box) where possible, in the condition it arrived in.

**Refund timing**: once the returned device is received and inspected, refunds go to the original payment method, typically within 5–7 business days. Don't promise a faster timeline.

Full legal terms live on the Return Policy page — link the customer there for specifics beyond this summary.
