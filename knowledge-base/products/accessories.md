# Accessories

One of six categories this store sells (MacBooks, iMacs, iPads, iPhones, Windows PCs, Accessories). This category is broader than the name suggests — it includes third-party peripherals (mice, hubs, docks) as well as smaller Apple devices sold as accessories rather than their own category (e.g. AirPods, Apple Watch).

**Never state a specific product, spec, price, or stock status from memory.** Always call `search_products` (category: "Accessories") for what's actually available right now.

## Condition grading

Accessories are graded on the same A–D scale as full devices where condition varies (e.g. a used AirPods Pro or Apple Watch) — see the grading breakdown in `products/macbooks.md`. Purely new/sealed third-party accessories (cables, hubs) may not carry a condition grade the same way; check the listing.

## When to suggest an accessory

Per the sales guidance in `sales/recommendations.md`: mention a relevant accessory when it naturally fits what the customer is already buying (a charger/case alongside a MacBook, a hub alongside a device with limited ports) — don't force it into every reply.
