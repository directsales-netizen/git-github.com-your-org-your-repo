## Condition grading (applies to every device we sell)

- **Grade A · Like New** — minimal signs of use, 85%+ battery health (where applicable), near-perfect screen, full original accessories included.
- **Grade B · Good** — light signs of use, 75–85% battery health, excellent screen.
- **Grade C · Fair** — visible signs of use, 65–74% battery health, good screen condition.
- **Grade D · Acceptable** — heavy signs of use, 50–64% battery health, fully functional, limited included accessories.

Battery-health grading applies to any device with a user-serviceable/measurable battery; for an all-in-one desktop like iMac, the grade instead weights screen condition, port function, and overall performance most heavily — don't assert a battery percentage for a desktop unless a specific listing states one.
