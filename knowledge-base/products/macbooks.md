# MacBooks

One of six categories this store sells (MacBooks, iMacs, iPads, iPhones, Windows PCs, Accessories).

**Never state a specific model, spec, price, or stock status from memory.** Current MacBook listings, their exact specs, condition grade, and price only exist in the live catalog — always call `search_products` (category: "MacBooks") to answer a real question about what's currently available. This file exists to explain the *system* (grading, what "refurbished" means here), not to list inventory, which changes.

## Condition grading (applies to every device we sell)

- **Grade A · Like New** — minimal signs of use, 85%+ battery health, near-perfect screen, full original accessories included.
- **Grade B · Good** — light signs of use (small scratches/minor marks), 75–85% battery health, excellent screen.
- **Grade C · Fair** — visible signs of use, 65–74% battery health, good screen condition, may show minor wear.
- **Grade D · Acceptable** — heavy signs of use, 50–64% battery health, fully functional, limited included accessories.

## What "professionally tested" means

Every MacBook is inspected before listing, covering the components the grade reflects: battery health, screen condition, keyboard/trackpad function, ports, and overall performance. See `support/warranty.md` for what that inspection is backed by.
