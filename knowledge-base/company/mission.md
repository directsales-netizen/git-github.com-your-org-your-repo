# Mission

Premium TechNoir delivers premium refurbished technology, professional repair services, AI-powered support, and sustainable technology solutions through an exceptional customer experience.

We extend the life of technology responsibly — every device we sell goes through professional testing and repair rather than being discarded, which means fewer raw materials mined, less energy spent manufacturing new devices, and less electronic waste headed to landfills.

Categories we sell: MacBooks, iMacs, iPads, iPhones, Windows PCs, and accessories. All professionally tested and condition-graded — see `support/warranty.md` and `products/` for grading details.
