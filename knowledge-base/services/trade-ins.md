# Trade-Ins

## How it actually works

A customer describes their device and its condition in free text through their account (Account → Trade-In). There's no instant/automatic quote — the team reviews the submission manually and, if approved, applies a store credit to the customer's account balance. There's no fixed trade-in value list to quote from.

## What the assistant should say

- Don't estimate a trade-in value — there isn't one to give without a manual review, and inventing a number would be exactly the kind of guess this assistant must never make.
- Direct the customer to submit the trade-in through their account, or offer to escalate to a human if they want to discuss it further before submitting.
- If they're not signed in, they'll need an account to submit a trade-in and see their credit balance.
