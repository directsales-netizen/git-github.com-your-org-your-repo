# Premium TechNoir Content & Marketing Strategy

## Social Media Content Mix

Target distribution:
- **40% Educational Content** – Buying guides, tips, maintenance, setup
- **30% Product Showcases** – New inventory, featured devices, deals
- **20% Behind-the-Scenes** – Testing, cleaning, packaging, team
- **10% Customer Stories** – Reviews, testimonials, use cases, company updates

## Packaging & Unboxing Experience

### Inside the Box
- Clean thank-you card with handwritten-style message
- QR code linking to: support, warranty registration, setup guide
- Quality assurance checklist (showing what was tested)
- Premium sticker with shield logo
- Branded tissue paper
- Optional: cleaning cloth or cable organizer
- Simple, minimal documentation
- Recycled/sustainable materials

### Brand Message on Materials
"Every device is professionally tested and responsibly sourced. Built to last. Here to help."

### Unboxing Experience Design
- Minimal, premium packaging
- Easy to open (no frustration)
- Quality materials (makes it feel special)
- Logo and brand colors visible
- Instructions simple and visual
- Thank you message prominent
- Encourages sharing/social media

## Sustainability Communication

### Factual Claims Only
- Devices kept out of landfills (with numbers)
- Years of device life extended (with calculations)
- E-waste prevented (kg measurements)
- Water/energy saved (compared to new production)

### Stories, Not Stats
- Customer stories of long-term ownership
- Device reuse journeys
- Refurbishment process transparency
- Supplier responsibility practices
- Recycling program details

### Avoid
- "Saving the planet" (too vague)
- Greenwashing language
- Unsubstantiated environmental claims
- Guilt-based messaging
